Ditto

Never lose a copied thought again.

Ditto saves your clipboard history, stores snippets you reuse, and reads text aloud — all from one keyboard shortcut.

Coming soon to the Mac App Store
⸻
Full clipboard history, one shortcut away
Pull up everything you've copied with a single keyboard shortcut.
Search past copies instantly
Type a few letters of what you're looking for and jump right to it.
Reusable snippets library
Save email replies, code blocks, addresses, and canned responses for one-shortcut access.
Read aloud in a natural voice
Hear any clipboard content read back — great for proofreading.
Custom hotkeys, anywhere
Summon Ditto from any app without breaking flow.
⸻

Native and lightweight

Built in SwiftUI. Ditto lives in your menu bar, uses almost no memory, and launches instantly. No Electron bloat, no background drain.

Private by design

Your clipboard history stays on your Mac. Nothing gets sent to a server, nothing syncs anywhere you didn't ask for. You decide what's saved and when it clears.

Made for the way you already work

If you've ever copied something, copied something else, and realized you needed the first one back — Ditto is for you. That moment of "wait, what did I just lose" disappears the first day you install it.
